exhibition in old main

art is such an amazing thing. a meaningful composition can be as simple as a line or as broad as an intricate mural in a cathedral. for this exhibit i chose to contribute four black and white pieces that have basic composures yet express much about what i try to convey in my work. all the ones i chose have a significant meaning that relates to the interconnectivity among the universe and how in tune we truly are but may not allow ourselves to be. art provides an outlet for me to play around with concepts like these and apply big ideas in simplified fundamental constructs. it is also such an intrapersonal tool, as everyone may interpret what they see differently. a piece could provide comfort to one but evoke discomfort to another. and it's less about what the art looks like and more so what it may represent. i hope however they speak to you that these works say something.
